Born: 1984, Hamamatsu, Japan
Lives and works in: Krefeld, Germany

Soya Arakawa, born in Hamamatsu, Japan, in 1984, is an artist weaving magic in Krefeld, Germany. Trained at Kanazawa College of Art and Kunstakademie Düsseldorf under Prof. Rita McBride, he dances between sculpture, painting, drawing, installation, and performance. His works blend choreography with spatial play, crafting vibrant installations and performance scores that come alive through song, dance, or music. Arakawa’s art is a playful exploration of cultural identity, fiction, and self-mythology, inviting you into a world of contemporary whimsy.
